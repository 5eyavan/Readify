How do recommendations work?

Recommendations are picked based on the filters you choose. You can refine them anytime.

Yes. You can save multiple books and update progress for each one.

Does Reading Flow need an account?

No account is needed. You can just choose a sound and start reading.

How is my email used?

Your email is only used for the newsletter and updates you agree to receive.
